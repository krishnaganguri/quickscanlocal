Frequently asked questions

What is QuickScan Local? ▼
QuickScan Local is a web-based application designed to enhance customer engagement by allowing easy check-ins, instant feedback collection, and loyalty rewards for returning customers.
How does the check-in process work? ▼
Customers can easily check in by scanning a unique QR code assigned to your business with their smartphones, eliminating the need for traditional sign-in sheets.
Can I see customer feedback after they check in? ▼
Yes! Customers have the option to leave feedback immediately after checking in, providing you valuable insights into their experience at your establishment.
Is there a rewards program integrated into QuickScan Local? ▼
Absolutely! QuickScan Local includes a built-in loyalty system where customers earn points for checking in or giving feedback. These points can be redeemed for discounts and special offers.
What features does the dashboard provide? ▼
The dashboard provides an overview of who is checking in, what type of feedback they are leaving, and how well your loyalty program is performing. It's designed to help you stay informed about customer interactions with your business.
